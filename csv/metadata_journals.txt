
Data description of the file "journals.csv"

This file contains the number of articles providing data and reproducible research between 2020-2021 in Journal of Archaeological Science (JAS), Journal of Archaeological Method and Theory (JAMT), Journal of Computer Applications in Archaeology (JCAA), and Plos ONE. 

Here the description of each field.

method (character): method for data sharing and reproducibility
JAS (numeric): percentage of articles  for each method of data sharing and reproducibility in JAS
JAMT (numeric): percentage of articles  for each method of data sharing and reproducibility in JAMT
JCAA (numeric): percentage of articles  for each method of data sharing and reproducibility in JCAA
Plos One (numeric): percentage of articles  for each method of data sharing and reproducibility in Plos One.
